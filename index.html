Fee Rates
Platform Fee (Upfront)
Charged as a one-off when a funder onboards a new project. Currently applied to Trilogy and Remara only — being discussed for broader rollout.
Revenue = Contract Value × Platform Fee %
Platform fee %0.20%
Apply to pipeline projects too?
Subcontractor Security Fee
Charged on the portion of each progress claim destined for subcontractors. Not every dollar goes to subs — the assumption is 80% of the build price is subcontractor work.
Revenue = Claim Value × Sub Allocation % × Subcon Fee %
Subcon fee %0.30%
Sub allocation % of claim80%
Early Release Fee
When a subcontractor requests funds before the contractual due date, Paid charges a daily fee. The model assumes 30% of users opt in and release an average of 30 days early.
Revenue = Claim Value × Adoption % × Days Early × 0.07%/day
User adoption30%
Avg days early30 days
Daily fee rate0.07%
Retention Yield
On every claim, Paid withholds 10% until the running retention balance reaches 5% of the total contract value — then withholding stops. The pooled retention earns yield while it sits in the system. Example: on a $50M contract, retention caps at $2.5M after $25M of claims.
Withheld = 10% per claim until balance = Contract Value × Retention Cap %
Yield = Running Balance × Yield Rate ÷ 12
Withholding rate per claim10%
Retention cap (% of contract)5%
Avg retention hold period15 months
Yield rate (p.a.)4%
Transitory Funds Yield
Every payment flows through Paid's platform — money comes in from funders and waterfalls out to subcontractors. The current assumption is funds sit for 5 days on average before leaving the system. Longer payment terms would increase this materially.
Yield = Total Platform Volume × (Days in System ÷ 365) × Yield Rate
Avg days in system5 days
Yield rate (p.a.)4%
