Model Assumptions & Revenue Mechanics

This page summarises how each revenue line is built up. All figures are pre-discount. Discount rates reflect probability-weighting applied by tier.

TIER 1 — Contracted (Certain)

Already generating actual revenue. Default discount: 55%.

Trilogy & Remara ($50M/qtr each)

  • Quarterly construction funding volumes of $50M each, ramping from 40% of run-rate in Apr-26 to 100% by Jan-27
  • Platform fee: 0.2% on total contracted volume
  • Subcontractor security fee: 0.3% × 80% subcontractor allocation
  • Dual retention yield: Pool 1 — funder withholds up to 5% of contract value from builder; Pool 2 — builder withholds up to 5% from subcontractors. Both pools earn 4% p.a. yield while held on platform
  • Transitory yield: All funds sit on platform avg 5 days before waterfalling — earns 4% p.a. on float
  • Early release fees: 0.07%/day × avg 30 days × adoption rate (ramps from 0% to 30% over 9 months as feature launches)

MBA / ABIC Contract Fees

  • Pool of ~2,000 contracts/yr at $179/contract avg (incl. GST)
  • Currently ~400 contracts active (20% penetration), ramping — controlled by MBA/ABIC penetration slider
  • Revenue taken directly from actual P&L and scaled proportionally with penetration

MBA / ABIC Financial Features

  • Average contract value: $2M
  • Current financial feature uptake: 1%, ramping to 5% target over 24 months
  • Generates: subcon security fee (0.3%×80%) + dual retention yield + transitory yield on the opted-in volume
  • At 5% uptake on 20% penetration: 20 contracts × $2M = $40M of construction volume on financial features

Retention Yield — Dual Pool
Two separate retention pools both sit in Paid's system and earn yield. Pool 1 (Funder → Builder): when a funder releases a progress claim, up to 5% of total contract value is withheld from the builder. Pool 2 (Builder → Subcontractor): when the builder pays subs, a separate 5% is withheld. Both pools accrue at 10% per claim until each hits its 5% cap. Combined, this means up to 10% of contract value sits in Paid earning yield.
Pool 1: Withheld = 10% per funder claim until balance = Contract Value × 5%
Pool 2: Withheld = 10% per sub claim until balance = Sub Contract Value × 5%
Yield = (Pool 1 + Pool 2) × Yield Rate ÷ 12
Withholding rate per claim10%
Retention cap (% of contract)5%
Retention is released when each project reaches practical completion — not modelled as a fixed hold period. The cap mechanic above controls how much accumulates.
Yield rate (p.a.)4%
Transitory Funds Yield
Every payment flows through Paid's platform — money comes in from funders and waterfalls out to subcontractors. The current assumption is funds sit for 5 days on average before leaving the system. Longer payment terms would increase this materially.
Yield = Total Platform Volume × (Days in System ÷ 365) × Yield Rate
Avg days in system5 days
Yield rate (p.a.)4%
